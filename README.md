  ## 1. Introduction

  EasyCals ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our calorie and macro tracking mobile application handles
  your information.

  **The short version:** We don't collect, store, or share your personal data. Everything you enter in EasyCals stays on your device.

  ---

  ## 2. Information We Collect

  ### 2.1 Information Stored Locally on Your Device

  EasyCals stores the following data **locally on your device only**:

  - **Food Log Data:** Foods you've logged, serving sizes, meal times, and dates
  - **Nutrition Information:** Calories, protein, carbohydrates, and fat amounts
  - **Custom Foods:** Foods you create and save to your library
  - **Recipes:** Custom recipes you build
  - **Weight Logs:** Weight measurements you choose to record
  - **Goals:** Your daily calorie and macro targets
  - **Preferences:** App settings like preferred units (kg/lbs)

  **Important:** This data is stored using Apple's secure on-device storage (SwiftData). It is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

  ### 2.2 Information We Do NOT Collect

  - ‚ùå Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number)
  - ‚ùå Location data
  - ‚ùå Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  - ‚ùå Usage analytics or crash reports
  - ‚ùå Contacts or photos (except when you use the camera for barcode scanning)
  - ‚ùå Health data from Apple Health or other apps

  ---

  ## 3. Camera Usage

  EasyCals requests camera access solely for scanning nutrition label barcodes to quickly add foods to your log.

  - **Camera images are processed instantly** and are never saved or stored
  - **Barcode data is only used** to search for nutrition information
  - **No photos are retained** after the barcode scan completes
  - **You can deny camera access** and still use all other features of the app

  ---

  ## 4. Third-Party Services

  ### 4.1 OpenFoodFacts API

  When you search for foods using the app's database search feature, we query the **OpenFoodFacts** public database to find nutrition information.

  - **What's sent:** Your search query (e.g., "chicken breast") or barcode number
  - **What's NOT sent:** Your personal data, device info, or usage patterns
  - **OpenFoodFacts Privacy:** OpenFoodFacts is a non-profit, open-source project. View their privacy policy at
  [openfoodfacts.org/privacy](https://world.openfoodfacts.org/privacy)

  ### 4.2 No Other Third Parties

  EasyCals does NOT use:

  - Analytics services (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics)
  - Advertising networks
  - Social media SDKs
  - Crash reporting services
  - Cloud storage or backup services

  ## 5. How We Use Your Information

  Since all your data stays on your device, we don't "use" your information‚Äî**you do**. The app uses your locally stored data to:

  - Display your food logs and nutrition summaries
  - Calculate total calories and macros for each day
  - Show your progress toward daily goals
  - Generate charts and historical views
  - Populate your custom food library

  **All processing happens on your device.** We have no access to this data.

  ---

  ## 6. Data Storage and Security

  ### 6.1 Local Storage

  - All data is stored using Apple's SwiftData framework
  - Data is encrypted by iOS when your device is locked (device-level encryption)
  - Data is isolated to the EasyCals app and cannot be accessed by other apps

  ### 6.2 Backups

  - If you use iCloud Backup or iTunes Backup, your EasyCals data may be included in your device backups
  - These backups are controlled by Apple, not by us
  - We recommend keeping device backups enabled to prevent data loss

  ### 6.3 Data Deletion

  - **Deleting the app** permanently removes all your data from your device
  - **There is no recovery** if you delete the app without backing up your device
  - We cannot recover your data because we never had access to it

  ## 7. Data Sharing and Disclosure

  **We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your data with anyone** because we don't have access to it. Your data stays on your device.
